About the community

The Research Cluster on FinTech & Digital Assets brings together economists, finance scholars, data scientists, and legal researchers to investigate one of the most consequential shifts in modern finance — the digitisation of money and assets.

We combine rigorous empirical methods with engagement across industry, regulators, and the open-source community. Our agenda spans the pricing and risk of crypto-assets, the design and welfare implications of central bank digital currencies, the microstructure of decentralised exchanges, the tokenisation of real-world assets, and the policy uncertainty surrounding digital finance. We believe credible research in this space must be open, replicable, and grounded in real market data.
