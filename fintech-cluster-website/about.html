Who we are & what drives us

A multidisciplinary group of researchers committed to open, rigorous, and policy-relevant scholarship on the digital transformation of finance.

Our mission

Digital assets and FinTech are no longer at the margins of finance — they are reshaping payments, capital markets, and monetary policy. Yet the evidence base remains thin and the discourse is often dominated by hype. Our mission is to produce credible, replicable research that helps academics, regulators, and practitioners understand the real economics of digital finance.

Three commitments.  (1) Open science — we publish code and data wherever possible. (2) Methodological rigour — careful identification, robust inference, and honest uncertainty. (3) Engagement — bridging academia, industry, and public policy.
