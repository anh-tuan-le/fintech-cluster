Research

Research themes & methods

Our work is organised around six interlocking themes spanning the economics, finance, technology, and regulation of digital assets.

Theme 01 · Asset Pricing

Crypto-Asset Pricing & Risk

We study the return dynamics, volatility, and tail risk of digital assets, and how they integrate into traditional multi-asset portfolios. Topics include factor models for crypto, jump risk, the diversification value of Bitcoin and Ether, and the role of policy uncertainty in driving returns.

Theme 02 · Microstructure

DeFi & Market Microstructure

Decentralised exchanges replace order books with automated market makers. We investigate price discovery, liquidity provision, impermanent loss, maximal extractable value (MEV), and how on-chain and off-chain markets interact.

Theme 03 · Monetary

CBDC & the Future of Money

Central bank digital currencies and stablecoins could reshape payments and bank funding. We model adoption, disintermediation risk, welfare effects, and the design trade-offs between privacy, programmability, and financial stability.

Theme 04 · Regulation

RegTech & Digital Asset Regulation

From MiCA to the Asia-Pacific, regulatory frameworks for crypto are emerging fast. We analyse how regulation affects market quality, the economics of compliance, and the use of technology (RegTech / SupTech) in supervision.

Theme 05 · Risk & Stability

Stablecoins, Contagion & Systemic Risk

Depegging events, run dynamics, and cross-protocol contagion in DeFi lending. We build early-warning indicators and stress-test the interconnections between digital and traditional finance.

Theme 06 · Tokenisation

Tokenisation of Real-World Assets

Bringing bonds, real estate, and commodities on-chain. We examine the valuation, liquidity, and legal-economic implications of asset tokenisation and the infrastructure required to support it.

Methods & data

Empirical finance

Panel and time-series econometrics, event studies, high-frequency analysis, and factor models.

On-chain data science

Blockchain analytics, transaction-graph methods, and machine learning on mempool and DEX data.

Theory & modelling

General-equilibrium and market-design models of money, payments, and exchange mechanisms.

Ongoing projects
  • DeFi Contagion Monitor — a real-time early-warning index for stablecoin and lending-protocol stress.
  • Crypto Policy Uncertainty Index — a news-based measure of regulatory uncertainty for digital assets.
  • RWA Tokenisation Database — cataloguing tokenised real-world asset issuances and their market performance.
  • CBDC Adoption Survey — household and firm-level evidence on willingness to use digital currency.
Open science. Where licensing allows, we publish our code and data so that results can be replicated and built upon. Many of these outputs appear in The Journal of FinTech and Digital Assets.
