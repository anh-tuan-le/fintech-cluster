About JFDA

JFDA serves as a global hub for advanced research shaping the digital finance revolution. The journal welcomes manuscripts that push conceptual boundaries, offer empirical insight, and provide solutions to real-world problems.

Its mission is to provide a rigorous, high-quality platform for academics, practitioners, policymakers, and industry leaders to exchange ideas, present empirical research, and engage in critical discussions that shape the future of finance in the digital era.

Aims & scope

JFDA publishes cutting-edge research that combines theoretical depth with practical relevance across financial technology and digital assets. It particularly welcomes original work on blockchain technologies, smart contracts, big data analytics, machine learning and text analysis, innovative payment systems, robo-advisory strategies, peer-to-peer lending, asset tokenisation, cryptocurrencies, open banking, and crowdfunding.
